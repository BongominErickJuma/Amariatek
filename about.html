Founder's Story

Linda Abeja

Director/Founder

In Uganda, a land of vibrant souls and dreams, Linda Abeja observed a quiet exodus. Talented individuals, brimming with potential, boarded flights in search of distant opportunities. Yet, the reality they met was harsh—a doctor’s healing touch reduced to cleaning, a lawyer’s articulate arguments replaced by cooking in foreign kitchens. This loss of talent, often called ‘brain drain,’ was a bleeding of the nation’s heart and future

Linda, a dedicated flight operations officer, refused to accept this as destiny. In 2019, she envisioned the Amariatek Foundation, its name meaning “I love you so much” in Luo, as a beacon for Uganda’s youth. She imagined a place where mentorship was abundant, guiding young Ugandans through the complexities of finance, entrepreneurship, and professional life.

Leaving her job behind, Linda forged a sanctuary of knowledge and empowerment. The Amariatek Foundation became a 8 testament to the belief that Uganda’s youth could thrive within their homeland. It redefined success—not by the distance travelled but by the milestones reached where they began.

The foundation’s story is woven from dreams, offering guidance, financial wisdom, and the promise of entrepreneurial and professional growth. It calls upon the youth to stay, to sow their brilliance in the soil that nurtured them, and to cultivate a future where the economy blossoms from the jobs they create.

Linda’s journey is a tale of transformation, echoing the aspirations of every young Ugandan who dares to dream. It’s an invitation to join a movement that seeks not just to change individual lives but to reshape the destiny of a nation. The essence of the Amariatek Foundation is a story of love, belief, and unwavering dedication to the untapped potential within.

Mission & Vision

Mission

To provide young individuals with the skills, mindset, tools, and confidence they need to reach their full potential.

Vission

A future where every young person has the opportunity to unlock their full potential, pursue their dreams, and make a meaningful impact in their communities.

Core Values

Our core values shape our identity and guide our behaviour; they guide us in delivering value to our stakeholders. Our values ensure that we meet the needs and goals of every individual we serve and work with.

Authenticity

We value the uniqueness and diversity of each team member and each individual we serve. We are true to ourselves and our mission.

Growth

We nurture the leadership potential and lifelong learning of each team member and each individual we serve. We provide the tools and opportunities for continuous improvement and innovation.

Passion

We love what we do and we do it with excellence. We are driven by our vision and we overcome any challenge with perseverance and resilience.

Personalization:

We tailor our work to the specific needs and goals of each individual we serve. We ensure that our approach is effective and relevant for each context and situation.

Holism:

We take a comprehensive approach to the development of each individual we serve. We address their academic, social, emotional, and career needs and aspirations. We measure our success by the positive transformation we witness in their lives.

Integrity

We uphold the highest standards of ethics and professionalism in our work. We are transparent, honest, and respectful in all our interactions and decisions.

Thematic Areas

Our thematic areas are the pillars that support this mission, each one a testament to our dedication to creating a brighter future for the youth of Uganda.

Education

From Learner to Doer and Earner Pathway. We champion a unique educational model that transitions learners into doers and earners. Our curriculum is crafted to provide practical life skills and entrepreneurial know-how, empowering students to turn knowledge into income and opportunities.

Livelihood

We focus on economic empowerment through job creation and entrepreneurship. Our programs support skill development, provide resources for starting businesses, and offer guidance in financial management, helping young people to build self-reliant and prosperous lives.

Youth Empowerment

Our empowerment initiatives provide platforms for leadership, innovation, and community engagement, enabling young Ugandans to become architects of their destiny.

Health And Welness

We address issues such as mental health, nutrition, and physical fitness, ensuring that young people have the resources and knowledge to maintain their well-being.

Climate Action

We are committed to climate action. Our initiatives promote environmental awareness, conservation, and sustainable practices among the youth. Fostering a generation that is conscious of and proactive about the health of our planet.
