foundation overview

The seed for the Amariatek Foundation was sown in January 2020. Founded on the backdrop of genuine concern for the issues plaguing the youth, the foundation quickly became a refuge for those seeking direction and empowerment.

The name “Amariatek” itself, deeply rooted in the Afrocentric Luo tradition, stands for “I love you so much,” underscoring the care, commitment, and passion that drive our actions.

With a dedication to mentorship and growth, the foundation strives to fill educational gaps often overlooked by traditional systems.

Through our diverse initiatives, Amariatek seeks to empower the youth, helping them not just to acknowledge their distinctive value but also to flourish across intellectual, emotional, social, and economic spheres.

Mission

At the Amariatek Foundation, we are dedicated to empowering Uganda's adolescents and youth with the education, resources, and support necessary to transform their lives and communities. Our holistic approach nurtures personal growth, economic empowerment, and social change, ensuring each individual has the opportunity to thrive and contribute positively to society.

Vision

A future where every young person has the opportunity to unlock their full potential, pursue their dreams, and make a meaningful impact in their communities.
